Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and how you can control them.

Gray Brands LLC (Bonafide Lyfe) · Effective Date: January 1, 2026 · Last Updated: May 1, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, understand how you use the site, and serve relevant advertisements. Cookies do not contain viruses and cannot access other files on your device.

Types of Cookies We Use

Always Active

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function. These enable your shopping cart, secure checkout, and session management. They cannot be disabled.

Optional

Performance & Analytics

Help us understand how visitors interact with our site via tools like Google Analytics. All data is anonymized and aggregated.

Optional

Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences such as language selection, region, and items in your wishlist to provide a personalized experience.

Optional

Marketing & Targeting

Used to show you relevant ads on other platforms. Set by our advertising partners including Meta (Facebook/Instagram) and TikTok.

Cookies We Set

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are set by third-party services we use:

  • Google Analytics: Web analytics to understand site traffic and behavior.
  • PayPal / Stripe / Shop Pay: Required for secure payment processing during checkout.
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram): Measures the effectiveness of our advertising on Meta platforms.
  • TikTok Pixel: Measures performance of our TikTok Shop and advertising campaigns.

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

How to Manage Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website, including your shopping cart and checkout process.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions → Manage and delete cookies

Opt-Out of Analytics

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Our Cookie Banner

When you first visit our site, a cookie consent banner allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our site.

Do Not Track

Our website does not currently respond to “Do Not Track” signals from browsers. You can manage your preferences through the browser settings described above.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
