Privacy Policy & Privacy Statement

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Gray Brands LLC (Bonafide Lyfe) · Effective Date: January 1, 2026 · Last Updated: May 1, 2026
Privacy Statement: Bonafide Lyfe, operated by Gray Brands LLC, is committed to protecting your personal information. We will never sell your data to third parties for profit. We collect only what is necessary to process your orders and improve your experience, and we give you full control over your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account & Order Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing and shipping address when you place an order or create an account.
  • Payment Information: Payment card details are processed directly by our payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, Shop Pay). We do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Communications: Messages, inquiries, and feedback you send through our Contact page or email.
  • Community Participation: Information you voluntarily share when joining our community, entering giveaways, or subscribing to our newsletter.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, clicks, referring URLs, and browser type collected via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser version.
  • Purchase History: Products viewed, added to cart, and purchased — used to personalize your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and returns.
  • Send order confirmations, shipping notifications, and customer service communications.
  • Send marketing emails and promotions (only with your consent; you may unsubscribe at any time).
  • Improve our website, products, and services through analytics.
  • Prevent fraud, protect security, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Personalize your shopping experience and product recommendations.

We will never use your information to make automated decisions that significantly affect you without your knowledge.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your data only with:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal, Shop Pay, Afterpay, Klarna — to process transactions securely.
  • Shipping Carriers: USPS, UPS, FedEx — to deliver your orders.
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics — to understand site usage in anonymized aggregate form.
  • Email Marketing: Our email platform provider — to send you newsletters and promotions you have opted into.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Bonafide Lyfe and its users.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies. For detailed information, please read our Cookie Policy. In summary:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the site to function (cart, session management).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for targeted advertising with your consent.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings or our cookie consent tool.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, secure server environments, and access controls. All payment information is processed by PCI-DSS compliant processors. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Order records are typically retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes. Marketing preferences are retained until you opt out.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email, or by contacting us directly.
  • Portability: Request a portable copy of your data in a commonly used format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at JessicajHallman@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell.
  • The right to delete personal information we have collected.
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights.

To submit a CCPA request, contact us at JessicajHallman@gmail.com or call (856) 266-4779.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you by email. Your continued use of our site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
