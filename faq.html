Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about shopping with Bonafide Lyfe.

📦 Orders & Shipping
Standard shipping typically takes 5–7 business days. Expedited shipping (2–3 business days) is available at checkout for an additional fee. Orders placed before 2 PM EST on business days are processed the same day.
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. No coupon code needed — the discount is applied automatically at checkout.
Absolutely. Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can also use the “Track Order” link in the navigation bar at the top of every page.
Currently we ship within the United States only. We are actively working on expanding international shipping — join our community to be notified when your country is added.
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, our fulfillment team begins processing and changes may not be possible. Please contact us immediately if you need to make a change.
🔄 Returns & Exchanges
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery on unused, unopened items in their original packaging. To initiate a return, please contact our support team with your order number.
Yes! We offer exchanges for items of equal or lesser value within 30 days of purchase. If the exchange item is higher in price, you will be charged the difference. Reach out to us to get started.
For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned: opened cosmetics, perfumes, skincare products, and items marked “Final Sale.” If you received a damaged or defective item, please contact us and we will make it right.
Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. The credit will appear on your original payment method within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.
💳 Payments
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Debit cards, PayPal, Shop Pay, Stripe, TikTok Pay, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Venmo, Afterpay, and Klarna. All transactions are encrypted and processed securely.
Yes! We offer two BNPL options: Afterpay (split into 4 interest-free payments) and Klarna (pay in 4, pay in 30 days, or monthly financing). Select your preferred option at checkout.
Absolutely. Our site uses SSL encryption and all payment information is processed by PCI-compliant third-party processors (Stripe, PayPal). We never store your full card details on our servers.
Sales tax is calculated at checkout based on your shipping address and applicable state laws. The amount shown at checkout is the final tax amount — there are no hidden fees.
✨ Products
We prioritize cruelty-free brands and clearly label which products are certified cruelty-free. We are continually working to expand our cruelty-free and vegan selections across all categories.
100% yes. Every product we sell is sourced directly from authorized distributors and brand partners. We have a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit goods.
We occasionally include free samples with orders based on availability. Keep an eye on our Community page and social media for sample promotions and giveaways.
